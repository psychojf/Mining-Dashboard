## How to Use EVE Mining Dashboard

### Managing Characters

Your characters will be detected automatically from existing game logs.
Click the **gear icon** (⚙) in the top-right corner to open the Config dialog.

- Check or uncheck characters to show or hide them on the main dashboard hub.
- Adjust the **Gamelogs Path** if your EVE logs are in a non-default location.
- Set the **Update Interval** (how often the dashboard reads new log data, minimum 250 ms).
- Set the **Default History Days** for the history window.
- Click the **UPDATE ORE DATA** button to fetch the latest EVE Online SDE ore volumes and compression ratios.


### Detachable Character Frames (NEW)

You can pop individual characters out of the main dashboard to freely arrange them on your screen:

- **Detach:** Click the pop-out icon (⇱) next to the character's profile name. The character will move into its own floating window.
- **Move:** Click and drag the custom title bar (e.g., `★ JF GELINAS`) at the top of the detached window.
- **Resize:** Click and drag the small resize grip (◢) in the bottom right corner of the detached window.
- **Auto-Fit:** Double-click the custom title bar to instantly snap the window back to its default, perfectly fitted size.
- **Session Breakdown (Accordion):** Click the `v SESSION BREAKDOWN v` button at the bottom of the frame to expand/collapse the detailed ore stats. 
- **Re-attach:** Click the close button (✕) on the detached window's title bar, or click the re-attach icon (⇲).
- **Persistence:** The app will automatically remember which characters are detached, along with their exact window sizes and screen coordinates, the next time you launch it!


### Configuring Ship Fittings

Right-click anywhere on a character's column (or inside their detached frame) and select **⚙ Ship Config** to open the fitting dialog.

- **Profiles:** Create multiple ship profiles (e.g., one for ore mining, one for ice, one for gas) using the `+ NEW`, `✎ RENAME`, and `✕ DELETE` buttons. Use the dropdown to switch between them. (You can also switch profiles quickly by clicking the gold `<Profile>` text directly on the dashboard).
- **Modules:** Configure up to 5 mining modules per profile. For each module, enter:
  - **Module Name** -- a label for your reference (e.g., "Strip Miner I")
  - **Yield (m3/cycle)** -- the m3 your module extracts per cycle (check your fitting window in EVE)
  - **Cycle Time (s)** -- the duration of one mining cycle in seconds
- **Drones:** Configure active mining drones, their yield, and cycle time.
- **Implant:** Toggle the Highwall MX-1005 implant for a +5% module yield boost.
- **Cargo Hold:** Set your ship's capacity to enable the visual cargo progress bar and time-to-full estimates.
- **Enable/Disable:** Use the checkboxes to toggle individual modules on or off. The **Theoretical rate** preview updates live. Click **SAVE** to apply.


### Start Monitoring

1. Set up your ship fitting and ensure your character is visible/detached where you want them.
2. Click **▶ START** under a character's column to begin tracking that session.
3. Mine as usual in EVE — stats, crits, cargo bars, and the actual m3/s rate will update in real time.
4. Click the **v SESSION BREAKDOWN v** toggle to see exactly how much of each ore you have mined.
5. Click **■ STOP** to pause tracking, or **↺ RESET** to clear all stats and cargo for that character.
6. Click **⏏ EMPTY** to reset just the cargo progress bar while keeping your session stats intact.


### Fleet Mode & Discord Reporting

Fleet Mode lets you report mining session statistics directly to a Discord channel via webhook.

1. Open **⚙ Config** and paste your Discord webhook URL into the **Webhook URL** field under the Fleet section.
2. Once you have active mining data for a session, two buttons will become available for that character:
   - **⎘ Copy to Clipboard:** Copies a plain-text session report (duration, crits, total m3, and ore breakdown).
   - **▲ Send to Discord:** Opens a preview dialog and posts the report directly to your Discord server.


### Viewing Mining History

Click the **◈ HISTORY** button at the bottom of the main dashboard.

- Enter the number of days to analyze and press Enter or click **↻ REFRESH STATS**.
- The history window shows total m3 mined per character with a per-ore breakdown based on your saved logs.
- **◈ EXPORT EXCEL:** Click this to generate a comprehensive `.xlsx` file containing a Summary sheet, Ore Pivot tables, and Daily breakdowns for each character.


### Configuration File

Settings are saved automatically to `mining_config.json` in the same folder as the script. This includes:

- All window positions and geometries (Main Dashboard, History, Config, and *each individual Detached Frame*).
- Which frames are currently detached.
- Character visibility selections.
- All ship profiles, modules, drones, and cargo configurations.
- Application settings (paths, intervals, webhooks).

*Note: Delete `mining_config.json` if you ever need to completely reset the application to its default state.*
